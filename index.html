Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026

Our Commitment

TaskMate is designed with a privacy-first architecture. Unlike many modern apps, we believe your productivity data should never leave your possession. This policy details how we ensure your data remains yours.

100% Local Storage

Everything you create—tasks, notes, and profiles—is stored in a secure, encrypted SQLite database on your device's internal storage.

Zero Transmission

TaskMate does not have a "cloud sync" feature. We do not maintain any servers to receive or store your personal information.

Permissions We Require

Requirement Reasoning
Microphone For the hands-free voice-to-text task creation feature.
Exact Alarms To ensure you receive notifications at the exact minute you set.
Photo Gallery To customize your profile locally with a picture of your choice.
Notifications Essential for task reminders and alerts.

Complete User Sovereignty

You are in full control of your data lifecycle. There are three ways to permanently delete your information:

App Settings > Clear All Data
System Settings > Apps > TaskMate > Clear Storage
Uninstall TaskMate (Deletes database permanently)
